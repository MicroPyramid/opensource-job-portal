Response Policy

We'll confirm your report with other users, data providers, or existing job information. Due to the volume of reports, we may not be able to respond to you directly. If approved, your change may take some time to appear on the site.

What You Can Report

You can report many types of issues with PeelJobs, such as problems with job listings, technical issues, feature requests, and any queries regarding our platform.

Writing an Effective Problem Statement

A problem statement is a clear, concise description of the issue(s) that need(s) to be addressed by a problem-solving team. It is used to center and focus the team at the beginning, keep the team on track during the effort, and is used to validate that the effort delivered an outcome that solves the problem statement.
