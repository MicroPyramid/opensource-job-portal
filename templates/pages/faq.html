FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

CANDIDATE :

Q : About peeljobs.com ?
A : Peeljobs.com is a job portal site where you can find your suitable jobs easily and fastly.

Q : How to apply for jobs?
A : According to your requirement you can direct apply jobs from social sites. you can see similar jobs also, if you find relevant you can apply as you want.

Q : How do i know i am being selected/shortlisted for a job ?
A : If you are selected, you will get a notification via email or given contact number.

Q : How do i submit my resume ?
A : When completing the details of the online application form, you will be asked to attach your CV, so make sure you have it ready.

Q : Can i apply more than one job ?
A : If you meet qualifications and experience necessary for that position then you may apply for more than one vacancy by completing the application requirements. Everyday new and active jobs are posted.

Q : If i do not find a suitable vacancy, what can i do ?
A : If you do not find a suitable job, we encourage you to see job details every day.
