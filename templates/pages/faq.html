Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using PeelJobs platform

Job Seekers

What is PeelJobs.com?

PeelJobs.com is a comprehensive job portal platform where you can find suitable job opportunities quickly and efficiently. We connect talented job seekers with top employers across various industries.

How do I apply for jobs?

You can apply for jobs directly through our platform. Browse jobs based on your requirements, view similar positions, and apply to multiple relevant opportunities with just a few clicks.

How will I know if I'm selected or shortlisted?

If you are selected or shortlisted for a position, you will receive notifications via email or through your registered contact number. You can also check your application status in your dashboard.

How do I submit my resume?

When completing your online application, you'll be prompted to upload your CV/resume. Make sure you have an updated resume ready in PDF or DOC format for easy submission.

Can I apply for multiple jobs?

Yes! If you meet the qualifications and experience requirements, you can apply for multiple positions. New and active job postings are added daily, giving you more opportunities.

What if I don't find a suitable job?

If you don't find a suitable position immediately, we encourage you to check our job listings regularly. You can also set up job alerts to be notified when relevant opportunities become available.

Is creating an account free?

Yes, creating a job seeker account on PeelJobs is completely free. You can browse jobs, apply for positions, and manage your applications at no cost.

Recruiters

How do I post a job on PeelJobs?

Create a recruiter account, complete your company profile, and use our job posting interface to create detailed job listings. Your jobs will be reviewed and published within 24 hours.

How can I manage applications?

Use your recruiter dashboard to view, filter, and manage all applications. You can shortlist candidates, schedule interviews, and communicate directly through our platform.

What are the pricing plans?

We offer flexible pricing plans based on your hiring needs. Contact our sales team for detailed information about our packages and enterprise solutions.

Companies

How do I create a company profile?

Register your company account, provide business details, upload your company logo, and create an engaging company description to attract top talent.

Can I manage multiple recruiters?

Yes, as a company admin, you can invite and manage multiple recruiters under your organization with different permission levels and access controls.

How do I track hiring metrics?

Access comprehensive analytics and reports through your company dashboard to track job performance, application metrics, and hiring funnel statistics.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble uploading my resume. What should I do?

Ensure your resume is in PDF or DOC format and under 5MB. Clear your browser cache and try again. If issues persist, contact our support team.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form, email us directly, or use the live chat feature available on our website during business hours.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
